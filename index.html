Instructions

The purpose of this application is to enable the user to experiment with the MBTI system and observe how the three core components of the system work together: the cognitive preferences, the cognitive functions, and the order of the cognitive functions. Read the descriptions and click on the cognitive functions to get started


Controls

  • Selecting Preferences: Selecting cognitive preferences directly is not possible in this application. Make note of the preferences that you may align with best, and try to achieve those selections with your selections of the cognitive functions

  • Selecting Functions: The cognitive functions may be selected both in the 'Overview' section and further down where the descriptions are. Selections may be made by clicking on a particular cognitive function. If the application is disallowing you from making a selection, it is because there are either too many selections (there may be only 2 at a time) or the particular selection violates the rules of the MBTI system

  • Deselecting Functions: Cognitive function selections may be removed by clicking again on the selection. The required order of removal is LIFO (last in, first out)
